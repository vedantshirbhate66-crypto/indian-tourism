Why Choose India for Tourism?

India, with its rich history spanning over 5,000 years, offers a unique blend of ancient traditions and modern vibrancy. Tourism here isn't just about sightseeing—it's about experiencing life in all its colors. From the spiritual aura of temples to the thrill of adventure sports, India caters to every traveler.

Key facts: India has 40 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, over 1,000 national parks, and hosts festivals like Diwali and Holi that light up the world. The economy benefits immensely, with tourism generating billions annually.

Types of Tourism

  • Cultural Tourism: Explore heritage sites like the Red Fort and Ajanta Caves.
  • Adventure Tourism: Trek in the Himalayas or raft in the Ganges.
  • Wellness Tourism: Relax with Ayurveda in Kerala.
  • Wildlife Tourism: Spot tigers in Ranthambore.
